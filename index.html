Welcome to Kyrgyzstan!

Kyrgyzstan is a rugged Central Asian country along the Silk Road,
the ancient trade route between China and the Mediterranean.
The Tian Shan mountains, which surround the old caravan route
and dominate the country, are home to snow leopards,lynx and sheep.
In the south, the millennia-old city of Osh has a huge, busy bazaar
that was once a stop on the Silk Road.
